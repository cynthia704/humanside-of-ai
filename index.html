Why now

AI is reshaping how people think, create, and decide — whether they're ready or not.

Most employees are handed powerful AI tools without the support, clarity, or judgment to use them safely and well. Organizations are moving fast — and their people are being left behind.

01

The rollout happened. The fluency didn't.

It's one thing to buy the licenses; it's another to make sure they're used well. The HumanSide of AI™ closes exactly that gap.

02

No shared standard for "good."

Without a common baseline for what good, responsible, and safe AI use looks like, every person quietly invents their own — inconsistent and risky.

03

This is a human problem first.

The hard part of AI isn't the prompt — it's the judgment, trust, and habits behind it. That's exactly where we focus.

70% people & process

Questions

Good to know

Do I need any technical or AI background?

None at all. Foundations is designed for any professional or manager — we start with a shared, plain-language baseline and build from there. It's tool-agnostic: you use whatever AI you already have access to.

What's the format and time commitment?

Three live, facilitator-led sessions of 3 hours each, run weekly or biweekly (about every three weeks), virtual or in-person, with light application on your real work between sessions.

Can my company enroll a team?

Yes — and we recommend it. There's a 10% group enrollment discount, up to 5 seats per company, and classes are capped at 50 participants. Bringing a team is the fastest way to build a shared standard.

When do the cohorts run?

Fall 2026 public cohorts: Cohort 1 meets Aug 13, Aug 27, and Sep 10; Cohort 2 meets Oct 1, Oct 15, and Oct 29. Seats are limited, so reserve early.

Do you offer enterprise delivery?

Yes. We deliver privately for organizations — ready-to-deliver or modular — and run deeper leadership programs. Email hello@thehumansideof.us and we'll tailor a plan.
