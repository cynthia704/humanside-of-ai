AI Literacy & Responsible Use

How AI works, what it doesn't do, how to talk about it clearly, and how to navigate risk in everyday work.

Prompting Mastery & Judgment

The variable that matters most in AI is not the prompt; it's the prompter.

Applied Workflow Integration

Participants apply AI to their real work: spotting the highest-value use cases, rethinking how work gets done, and building safeguards for quality.
